Tomato and Egg Stir Fry

Description

Tomato and Eggs Stir Fry is a popular and simple Chinese dish that is made by stir-frying tomatoes and beaten eggs together. The dish is known for its sweet and savory flavor, as well as its bright red color.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • salt
  • 3 green onions
  • 4 tomatoes
  • cooking oil of your choice
  • soysauce

Steps

  1. Add the 4 eggs into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add a quarter teaspoon of salt to the mxing bowl and beat the bowl with chop sticks.
  3. Chop 3 green onions and set aside.
  4. Cut 4 tomatoes into half inch wide wedges.
  5. Heat wok on high heat and add 1 tablespoon cooking oil to it.
  6. Pour beaten egges into wok once it is smoking hot.
  7. Scramble the eggs so it is cooked evenlt but not too much so the egg is still loose. remove egg from wok whehn it is 3/4 done.
  8. Turn heat back on to medium heat with wok and add 1 tablespoon of oil to it.
  9. Add half of chopped green onions until you can smell the aroma.
  10. Add tomatoes to wok along with 2 teaspoons of soysauce along the edge of the wok.
  11. Add 1/4 teaspoon of salt to wok and stir
  12. When bottom of wok is shimmering and bubbling add the egg back into the wok and break it into the tomato.
  13. Add the rest of the green onion to the wok.
  14. After a minute or so it is done! serve and enjoy.
