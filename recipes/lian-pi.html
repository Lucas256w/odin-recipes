Lian Pi

Description

Mapo Tofu is a popular Chinese dish made with soft tofu and ground meat, usually pork, in a spicy sauce made with fermented black beans, chili paste, and Sichuan peppercorns.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground pork
  • 2 tablespoons of Szechuan peppercorn
  • 1 tablespoon of broad bean paste
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of water
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 6-8 dried chili pepper
  • 1 cup of vegetable stock or water
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the ground pork, Szechuan peppercorn, broad bean paste, soy sauce, garlic, cornstarch, and water.
  2. Heat a wok or large pan over high heat and add the vegetable oil.
  3. Once the oil is hot, add the dried chili pepper and stir fry for a few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the pork mixture and stir fry until the pork is cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the vegetable stock or water and bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in the green onions and cook for another minute.
  8. Serve hot over steamed rice.
